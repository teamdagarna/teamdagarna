One day where ambitious students meet potential employers

TEAM is one of the largest and most visited career fairs in Sweden where ambitious students meet potential employers. This year, TEAM will be arranged digitally through our website teamdagarna.com.

The fair is a collaboration between the student association of Industrial Engineering and Management and the Business and Economics programme (ELIN). The aim is to provide students in engineering and business with opportunities to get in touch with leading companies from all over Sweden.
A few of the opportunities we offer students include:

Interviews

TEAM offers students the possibility to meet business representatives from a range of companies during a private conversation about jobs and future possibilities. This is a great way to establish a connection with the company of your choice, but should by no means be considered a job interview. Applications for so called “mock interviews” will open in advance, and the interviews themselves take place digitally on the 10th of November. Keep an eye out for information regarding application deadlines and companies that offer interviews!


Events

Due to the ongoing pandemic, there will be a limited amount of events arranged. More information regarding planned events will be announced soon.
